Assuming A Web Presence

It's never too late to start assuming a web presence, and it is as important as ever. Having your own web outlet is not just about existing on the inter-connected world it is also about controlling the message; your message.

For how people and organisations perceive you and your personal brand is increasingly dependant on what can be found on the web. Having your own blog and social media accounts is one way to influence what exists about you on the web.

Setting up a blog or a website is the first step towards achieving more control your personal brand and image.
